2. Research Growth Mindset

• Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

A growth mindset is having an understanding that it is possible to overcome obstacles when learning a new skill by having persistence and determination. It is relevant to learning as it is more beneficial to learning compared to a fixed mindset where fixed mindsets can influence our development of skills and learning in a bad way.

• In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

The fact that it is possible to improve/learn better simply by shifting your mindset is quite amazing to me. It has changed my outlook on learning and wish to implement it more in my everyday life.

• How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

I can integrate growth mindset by reminding myself each day that I am here to learn along with others doing the same task. I will also ask others for help if I’m stuck in something too.
