• What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

Values and self awareness during learning help to create a better focus when it comes to learning, by instilling a good ethic it creates a better drive towards learning while self awareness helps to understand the strengths and weaknesses of one's skills even in programming. Having Empathy for others helps to understand that learning is not just a one person endeavour. By helping others it can create friendships/relationships and share knowledge/information.
